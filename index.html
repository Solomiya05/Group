Project history

In November 2009, the premiere of the first song, “Meaning.net”, took place on radio and television, and on December 29, 2009, Pianoboy played his first full-length solo concert in Kiev, where a video for this song was also presented.

Pianoboy

Pianoboy is a solo project of Ukrainian musician Dmitry Shurov, former keyboard player of Okean Elzy, Esthetic Education, Zemfira. Established in 2009. Performs songs in Ukrainian, Russian and English. Dmitry's own sister Olga Shurova also takes part in the project.

Project history

In March 2012, Pianoboy won the "Chart dozen" award from the Russian radio station "Nashe Radio" thanks to the song "The Witch" in the "Opening of the Year" nomination. In May 2012, the band released its debut album "Simple Things". The album was released on the label Lavina Music. In August 2012, Pianoboy, along with Bumbox and TNMK, recorded the cover for "For You" by Micah and Jumanji for a live tribute, and in January 2013, they released a video for this cover.
